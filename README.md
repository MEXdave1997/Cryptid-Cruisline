# Cryptid Cruiselines!

Repository for the game *Cryptid Crusilines*, a Visual Novel set in the Femios verse! 

The game is built using [Ren'Py](https://www.renpy.org/), the open source game engine written in Python!

---

## Plot 
Fans of the hit popular series, Jack Griffin & the Coven Girls form a college club and with their fundraiser, they manage to gather enough to pay for a cruise set for their last month of Summer! In this adventure, they end up meeting the cast & crew of the show. Their dreams come true as the Coven girls are actually WITCHES, Jack Griffin really IS invisible, and monsters are real, and in more ways than one! Watch as three friends try to keep the peace between their fellow club members.
